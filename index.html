Frequently Asked

What is Wellness House?

+

Wellness House is Willpower's flagship gathering — a full-day, invite-led experience at the intersection of wellness, performance, and consumer brands. Now in its 3rd year, it brings together founders, operators, athletes, and brand leaders for panels, movement, recovery, and community.

When and where is the event?

+

Friday, March 13, 2026 in Austin, TX. Full day event running 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM, followed by an evening social on the lawn.

How do I attend?

+

Attendance is approval-based. Click "Apply to Attend" to submit your application. General Admission is $300, with limited Early Bird tickets at $150.

Who should attend?

+

Founders, senior operators, and leaders from high-growth wellness, CPG, and consumer brands. Athletes, creators, and teams shaping performance culture. Retail, investment, and media partners.

What's the vibe?

+

Think premium, not stuffy. The room is curated for quality over quantity — real conversations, not sales pitches. Start the morning moving, spend the day learning from founders, and end the afternoon connecting over music and recovery activations.

How can my brand get involved?

+

We collaborate with a select group of brands, speakers, and partners. Interested in sponsorships, programming, or media collaboration? Reach out via the "Partner With Us" button or email bill@willpowerhq.com.

How many spots are left?

+

We're approaching capacity. With over 120 applications already received and limited space, we recommend applying soon to secure your spot.

What is Willpower?

+

Willpower brings together performance, wellness, and community through curated events and high-impact collaborations. With 4,000+ leaders across Austin and NYC and 24+ curated gatherings hosted, Wellness House is our flagship annual gathering.
